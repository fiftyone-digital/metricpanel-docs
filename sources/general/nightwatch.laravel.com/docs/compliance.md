# Source: https://nightwatch.laravel.com/docs/compliance

We’re committed to providing a secure and compliant platform for your Laravel applications. We understand that security and compliance are critical for your business, and we’re actively working towards achieving industry-leading certifications. This page outlines our current compliance roadmap and provides transparency into our ongoing efforts.

## 

[​](https://nightwatch.laravel.com/docs/compliance#current-status-and-upcoming-milestones)

Current status and upcoming milestones

We’re excited to share that we’re actively pursuing the following compliance certifications:

- **SOC 2 (Service Organization Control 2):** Currently we are working on obtaining a SOC 2 Type 1 report, this report will be available by request in the coming weeks. This certification demonstrates our commitment to maintaining a robust security posture and adhering to stringent industry standards.
- **ISO 27001:** Following the completion of SOC 2, our next major compliance initiative will be achieving ISO 27001 certification. This internationally recognized standard for information management systems (ISMS) will further solidify our dedication to protecting your data and ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our platform. We aim to deliver full ISO 27001 compliance sometime during H2 of 2025.

## 

[​](https://nightwatch.laravel.com/docs/compliance#what-this-means-for-you)

What this means for you

Our commitment to these compliance standards means you can trust Laravel Nightwatch to handle your sensitive data with the highest level of security and care. These certifications demonstrate our dedication to:

- **Data Security:** Protecting your data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.
- **Availability:** Ensuring the continuous operation of our platform and minimizing downtime.
- **Confidentiality:** Maintaining the privacy of your data and preventing unauthorized access.
- **Compliance:** Adhering to industry best practices and regulatory requirements.
