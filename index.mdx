MetricPanel gives you clear, actionable analytics without compromising your visitors' privacy. Install a lightweight tracking script or JavaScript SDK, define goals and funnels, and attribute revenue from Stripe, Polar, LemonSqueezy, or Shopify back to the campaigns and pages that drove it.

## Start here

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your website">
    Create a MetricPanel account, open the dashboard, and add the website you want to track. Copy your **website ID** from the setup flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the tracking script">
    Add the hosted snippet to your site layout so it loads on every page — or use the JavaScript SDK for framework-managed initialization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your first event">
    Open your site in a browser and verify a live visitor appears in MetricPanel. Check that your first pageview and a custom event are recorded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build goals, funnels, and revenue">
    Turn tracked events into measurable outcomes. Create goals for signups and purchases, build funnels to find drop-off points, and connect a payment provider to attribute revenue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
